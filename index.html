About myself

Hello! I'm Elena Šyvokaitė.

I'm a passionate young person with a strong drive for learning new things.

Currently, I work in administration for a children's literacy charity and in my spare time I am studying for an IGCSE in French. Previously, I lived and worked in France where I taught English to high school students while also trying to uncover my passions that would pay the rent.

When I moved back to London after my placement, and after a summer of getting reaccustomed to the rainy days and grey skys, I was called in for jury service where, by word of mouth, I came across Code First: Girls.

I became interested in coding and explored my options. I began learning Python with Cf:G, watching programmers on YouTube, starting freecodecamp's online curriclum, attending tech events to speak to professionals in the field and finally trying to build something on my own.
So, I created this website to showcase my passion and my skills.
